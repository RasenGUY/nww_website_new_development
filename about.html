our story

The New World International English Center is an ESL school for kids of all ages set in the northern part of Vietnam. Located in the main city of Thai Binh district, New world tries its best to provide very affordable English lessons to Vietnamese children in and around Thai Binh City. It achieves this through the application of an array of games, workshops and ESL lessons, with the help from a number of foreign ESL teachers. New World sets itself apart from other English centers in Vietnam in that we host the largest number of foreign English speaking volunteers in Thai Binh City.

5 years ago we decided to open the first English center in Kiến Xương, Thai Binh as a result of an increase in demand of ESL courses. A few months later we opened a new center in Thái Thụy, Thai Binh. After that another center was opened in Tien Hai, however this center was not successful and was thus closed. Our director then proceeded to open another school in Thai Binh city (the city center of Thai Binh) this center was really successful and after three years of operation the school relocated to another location in Thai Binh.

our mission

Our mission is to provide very affordable English lessons for students regardless of their social background (rich and poor). New World recognizes the importance of the English language and the opportunity it provides for people in the labor market that’s why we continue to work hard provide children with the opportunity to learn English. Our long term goal is to reach the ethnic minorities in the outskirts of Thai Binh and provide English lessons to all.
