our story

The New World International English Center is an ESL school located in Thai Binh district (North Vietnam). We provide affordable classes to kids of all ages. This is achieved through the application of an array of games, workshops and ESL lessons, with the help from a number of foreign ESL teachers.

We opened the first English center in Kiến Xương, Thai Binh. Our second school is located in Thái Thụy, Thai Binh. Our main center which is them most successfull, is located in Thai Binh city and it's our main center of operations.

our mission

Our mission is to provide very affordable English lessons for students regardless of their social background (rich and poor). New World recognizes the importance of the English language and the opportunity it provides for people in the labor market that’s why we continue to work hard provide children with the opportunity to learn English. Our long term goal is to reach the ethnic minorities in the outskirts of Thai Binh and provide English lessons to all.
